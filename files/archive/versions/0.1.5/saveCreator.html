Region Information

The different regions available in Rising Dawns are very different, both in abundant resources and enemies. Whichever you choose is going to greatly influence the rest of your gameplay. My suggestion is that you take at least a scan over the region information sheet here.

Patreon and Donation

The Rising Dawns developer team is against adding advertisements to our game or even the save creator. This means that our only way of income from this game is our patreon. You can get early notice of updates before they happen, early play-testable copies of the new updates, and even an early art showcase every now and again. Plus, you get to make sure that us as developers can buy better or new equipment to make Rising Dawns better. Here, take a look.

Report Issues

Issues in an alpha, or even beta, game is inevitable. So that makes reporting these issues--no matter how small and seemingly inconsequential--is integral to the continued development of Rising Dawns. GitHub has a fancy little feature called 'Discussions', where the community of a project can report issues or even just talk about things. If you find anything you don't think is part of the game, or want to suggest a feature, post something on there, or contact us via our Email.

Desktop Game?!

Browser games are cool and all, but desktop apps are generally where most games find their success, and most consumers find the most interest. We as a devteam, and as consumers, do think that an app is definitely needed for Rising Dawns. However, our game is still in the earliest of the earliest stages. Our current plan is to develop the game until we fully integrate graphics, and complete some features we've dreamed up. Once this happens, we'll convert the whole game into the decided language (C#, C++, or another if the plan changes). There's some more in-depth information here.
